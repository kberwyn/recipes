Ingredients:
2 slices large thin crust Papa Johns pizza, pepperoni
1 hot dog, Costco
10 oz chili, any variety (preferred Stagg or use recipe in this repo)
1 handful shredded mexican cheese
Sriracha or other hot sauce

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Prep:
Wrap hot dog in paper towl and microwave, 30 sec
Place slices of pizza on top of each other, face up and with the point of each slice on top of the crust of the other
Place hot dog on slices
Ladle chili on that shit
Put cheese on it
Hot sauce to taste

Shine on you crazy diamond
